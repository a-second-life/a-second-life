It’s not old.
It’s ready for a second life.

Every year, billions of smartphones are forgotten in drawers, abandoned, or thrown away — even when they could still be useful.

Some have a cracked screen. Some have weak batteries. Others are simply considered outdated.

But outdated does not mean useless.

According to the WEEE Forum, around 5.3 billion mobile phones became potential electronic waste in a single year. Most of them still contained valuable and functional technology.

A_SECOND_LIFE starts from a simple idea: before recycling, we can reuse.

An old smartphone can become a music player, a smart home device, a security camera, a digital object designed to live differently.

Reuse before recycle.

Technology should not disappear just because it is no longer new. We believe existing devices deserve another chance, another function, another story.
