UNFINISHED.

Every year, millions of smartphones are forgotten in drawers, replaced, abandoned or thrown away. But a device does not lose its value just because it is no longer new.

A_SECOND_LIFE gives old technology a new purpose, transforming forgotten devices into useful, beautiful and meaningful objects.

WHY REUSE MATTERS

Many phones still work. Some only have a cracked screen, a weak battery, limited memory, an old operating system or small defects that make them less attractive as personal devices.

But inside each one there is still technology: a screen, speakers, sensors, a camera, a processor, connectivity and energy already spent to produce it. Throwing it away too early means wasting all of that.

Before recycling, we can imagine. Before replacing, we can transform. Before forgetting, we can give technology another role in our lives.
